# Sandy Vavoom: Brand & Website Manifest (v3)

This document serves as the master reference for the Sandy Vavoom (Sandra Landeros Thomas) brand architecture and website development. 

## 1. Brand Architecture Overview
**Master Brand:** Sandy Vavoom  
**Persona:** The Multi-Hyphenate Creative Force.  
**Structure:** House of Brands (Umbrella model).

### Sub-Brands:
1.  **Leopard Lounge (The Duo):** Intimate, mid-century modern, cocktail vibes. Serving the Central Coast with smooth Bossa Nova and jazz.
2.  **RetroActive (The Rock Band):** High-energy, classic rock with a dark, atmospheric/gothic edge. 
3.  **GoGirl Wine Driver (Winery Services):** A specialized "Designated Driver" service based in **Paso Robles**. Sandy drives the client's vehicle, providing expert-led tours across the **Central California Coast**.

## 2. Website Content Map

### Page: Home (The Portal)
* **Headline:** The World of Sandy Vavoom.
* **Sub-headline:** Performance. Presence. Provisions.
* **Geo-Tagline:** Based in the heart of Paso Robles, serving the Central California Coast.

### Page: GoGirl Wine Driver
* **The Concept:** "You relax, I'll drive." A premium designated driver service for your own vehicle.
* **Location Focus:** Proudly serving **Paso Robles**, **Templeton**, **San Luis Obispo**, and the broader **Central Coast**.
* **Aesthetic:** Clean, professional, airy, vineyard-inspired greens and creams.
* **Key Content Sections:**
    * **How it Works:** Clarify the "Your Car, My Driving" model—cost-effective, safe, and personalized.
    * **Local Expertise:** Highlight Sandy's deep knowledge of Paso Robles' distinct wine regions (Adelaida District, Willow Creek, etc.).
    * **The Driver:** Highlight safety, reliability, and local "Central Coast" insider status.
* **Call to Action:** * **Primary:** [Link to Booking Calendar]
    * **Secondary:** Follow on Instagram [@GoGirlWineDriver]

### Page: Leopard Lounge & RetroActive
* **The Performance Presence:** Mentioning availability for local Central Coast venues, tasting rooms, and private vineyard events.

### Page: About (Sandra Landeros Thomas)
* **The Story:** Bridging the gap between the precision of a professional driver and the artistry of a stage performer.
* **The Connection:** Sandra's roots in the Central California Coast inform her work—from the rhythm of the local music scene to the terroir of the Paso Robles wine country.
